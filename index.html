Adding Signature Chat to Microsoft Outlook

Add Zoho SalesIQ Signature chat in your reply emails sent from Microsoft Outlook, and swiftly get connected with your customers in real time.

  1. In Microsoft Outlook, click Settings icon >View all Outlook settings >Mail >Compose and reply.

​

  1. Now, in the Zoho SalesIQ ->Settings ->Websites ->Live chat for emails ->signature chat code section, tap on the click here below the signature chat code and copy the code from the Link URL section.
  2. In the Email Signature section, enter the text you wish to add as the sig

  1. Then, choose the text and click the link option and paste the link. And, click Save .

  1. Now, you can navigate to the create email section, and compose your email. Then click More options icon at the bottom and Insert the Signature at the end and send the email.

  1. When the recipient click on the signature link, they will land on the Email recipient page. Learn how to setup the email recipient page
